How to play Lotto Golden Chance

This game is easy to play on the Internet. You need a computer or laptop, a tablet device or smartphone and fast Internet connection. Just follow this instruction and you will understand this fun process:

  • The game has its own official Golden Chance lotto site. It is https://www.winnersgoldenchance.com/. Go to this website to place bets on the web.
  • There is the main menu on the top just like on many other websites. It says ‘Play,’ ‘Payments,’ ‘Contact Us’ and ‘FAQ.’ You are advised to read all the questions and answers, learn about the payment process and access online lotto in Nigeria though ‘Play’ menu button.
  • Register a profile. You will be asked to enter your real cell phone number. This is how you are able to play the games on your phone. You must be at least 18 years old. Otherwise, you are not allowed playing online or on the phone.
  • After you have created your profile you need to log in with your login details (login and password).
