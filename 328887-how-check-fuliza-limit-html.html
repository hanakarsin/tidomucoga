How to check Fuliza limit

With the successful implementation of M-Pesa, a mobile money platform where customers can send money via their mobile phones, the telecommunication giant introduced an additional feature to its M-Pesa customers, Fuliza. With different customers having different limits, how to check Fuliza limit is an area in which many customers have questions. What is Fuliza? Who can access this service?

With the advancement of mobile money services to the most remote areas of the country and the daily transactions of many Kenyans, different challenges arose from this facility. The inability to complete an M-Pesa transaction because of the lack of enough funds was one such challenge and thus Fuliza was introduced. With this service, it is quite easy to continue with day to day transactions and not be stuck because of insufficient funds. So, can you withdraw Fuliza? What exactly are the steps on how to check Fuliza limit?

What is Fuliza?

It is an overdraft service that allows M-Pesa customers who have registered for the option to complete their M-Pesa transactions when they have insufficient funds in their M-Pesa account. It provides a prompt on the screen when you try to perform any transaction with insufficient funds and allows you to choose whether or not you wish to proceed and Fuliza or cancel the transaction. If you select to proceed, it will clearly indicate the extra amount which will be credited to you and allow you to proceed with the transaction. This means that Fuliza is not an M-Pesa loan but a continuous overdraft service just to allow you to complete a transaction.

How do I opt-in?

To join, you need to be an M-Pesa registered customer with an active Safaricom line. This means that you will have to have been a Safaricom customer for more than 6 months in order to qualify to be an M-Pesa user and consequently a Fuliza user. If you have achieved this basic requirement, all you need to do is dial *234# and select Fuliza M-Pesa option to opt-in.

What are the charges

Charges for using this service are broken down into categories with different categories having different charges associated with them. The rates for the services are given as follows:

  • Ksh 0-100 one-time back fee of Ksh 2
  • Ksh 101-500 1% access fee and a daily fee of Ksh 5
  • Ksh 501-1000 1% access fee and a daily fee of Ksh 10
  • Ksh 1001-1500 1% access fee and a daily fee of Ksh 20
  • Ksh 1501-2500 1% access fee and a daily fee of Ksh 25
  • Ksh. 2501-70000 1% access fee and a daily fee of Ksh 30

You can also query charges for specific amounts. To do this, dial *243# and select the Fuliza option then choose the query charges option. Input the specific amount you wish to find out and submit it. You will receive an SMS with the details concerning that amount.

How to check Fuliza limit

Does Fuliza have a limit? Yes. Different customers have different limits. To check your limit, dial the USSD code *234#, choose Fuliza and select the option to check your limit. After the request is received by Safaricom you will receive an SMS detailing how much you can borrow and your maximum limit.

How do I repay?

The repayment process is automated. Any funds received or deposited in your M-Pesa account will automatically be used to clear your outstanding balance. It is recommended that you clear your Fuliza M-Pesa balance within 30 days. Past the period, you will not access the service if you have any unpaid balance. This will, however, be restored upon the full repayment of your balance. The sooner you repay your balance, the better it is for you.

How to increase Fuliza limit

In order to increase your limit, all that is required is that you continue using Safaricom and M-Pesa services frequently and make sure you repay Fuliza in time by topping up your M-Pesa account. This will allow your limit to gradually increase.

Why is my Fuliza limit zero?

You cannot access Fuliza if any of the conditions below are not met:

  • You have not been on M-Pesa for at least 6 months.
  • You are listed with the credit reference bureau (CRB).
  • You have defaulted your Mshwari loan.

    • Any of the conditions listed will cause you to have a zero limit. A detailed list of all the Fuliza terms and conditions can be found on the Safaricom website or the Commercial Bank of Africa website.

    How do I opt-out?

    To opt-out, simply dial *234# and choose the Fuliza M-Pesa option. Once in the menu, choose from the list the option to opt-out of the service. There are no charges for ending this service and you can re-activate this feature any time you wish.

    You can continuously monitor your Fuliza status by getting a mini-statement by simply dialing *234#, selecting Fuliza M-Pesa and choosing the option to receive a statement. It will provide detailed information about your current and previous status of the service.
