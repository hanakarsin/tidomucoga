ThunThun Skittles - Age, Family, Bio

Content creator named Alana who is known for her comedic lip syncs and skits. She has over 8 million fans on TikTok and also posts content to YouTube and Instagram. She has documented herself trying things for the first time, experimenting with makeup and reacting to K-pop and TikTok videos. 

Before Fame

She began creating video content in 2013 for YouTube and Vine.

Trivia

Some of her most popular videos on YouTube include reaction videos focused on TikTok. She has over 120,000 followers on Instagram. A YouTube video of her trying to play Roblox for the first time in June of 2019 has received over 490 thousand views. 

Family Life

She has a sister.

Associated With
