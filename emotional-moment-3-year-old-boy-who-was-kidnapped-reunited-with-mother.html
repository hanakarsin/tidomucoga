Emotional Moment 3-Year-Old Boy Who Was Kidnapped Reunited With Mother

 Posted on August 4, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  165 words  |  Enda Jantzen

A video showing the moment a 3-year-old boy, who was abducted by a nanny in Abuja, reunited with his mother has surfaced on social media.

This comes after an Abuja-based business owner, via her Instagram Monday, July 10, 2023, said the nanny left the Gwarimpa area of the FCT and never returned.

The suspect allegedly works for a kidnap syndicate, and gained access to the little boy’s home disguising as a house help.

Calling for help, the mother wrote on social media, “Please help find this baby and pray for his safe return. Taken by his nanny from an estate in Gwarimpa Abuja. Please be on the look out and pray. The boy’s mum is battling cancer.. help please.”

The kidnappers reached out and demanded for N15million but the boy’s sick mother pleaded with them to accept N5million which they rejected.

After bargains, the boy’s parent paid the ransom and the child was released.
