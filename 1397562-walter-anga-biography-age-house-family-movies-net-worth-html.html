Walter Anga biography

Who is Walter Anga? He is a Nigerian actor born on 12th January 1978 in Port-Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. His full name is Walta Angapukotanka Kalada. He is the son of Stephen Angapukotanka (father), and Evelyn Angapukotanka (mother).

Anga is the third child in a family of five children. He was raised by his parents, together with his siblings in Ogu-Bolo local government area of Rivers state in the southern part of Nigeria.

Walter Anga education

He attained his First School Leaving Certificate and West African Senior School Certificate in River State. He then enrolled at the University of Port-Harcourt for a degree in Theater Arts and graduated in 2002.

Career

Anga began his career after completing his university education. He landed his first role as a supporting character in the film, Labista. He starred alongside Ernest Asuzu, Jerry Amilo and Emma Ehumadu. It was almost two years before he landed another role.
