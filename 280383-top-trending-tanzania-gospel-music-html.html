Top Trending Tanzania gospel music

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1489 words  |  Lourie Helzer

Many of us have grown up listening to Tanzania gospel music. Some did so without knowing that some of the artists we have listened to are from Tanzania. The gospel music in Tanzania stands out mainly because of the pure Swahili language by many artists. This is in comparison to the gospel music in other countries that may mix several languages.

The gospel music industry in Tanzania has a good number of artists. If you are in search of the best trending songs of all time in Tanzania’s gospel circles, here is a Tanzania gospel music list.

1. Upendo wa Yesu - Upendo Nkone

Subscribe to watch new videos

In case you forgot how this one goes, listen to it above. ‘Upendo wa Yesu’ means the love of Jesus which is a title of a gospel song done by Upendo Nkone. This female artist is a household name in Tanzania’s gospel scene. In this song, Nkone basically reminds Christians of the unfailing love of Christ our maker. This love surrounds us daily no matter where we are or what we are doing. The beat of the song is amazing and so are the lyrics that are easy to sing along to. It is also an uplifting song that preserves our faith as Christians. It also reminds us that indeed, there is a higher power than us whose love is unconditional.

2. Utamu wa Yesu - Rose Muhando

Subscribe to watch new videos

Apart from the lyrics and wonderful video, the dance moves from the dancers propelled it to become a favorite for many. Rose Muhando has always been known to be a powerhouse across gospel music Tanzania. She did not disappoint in this song either. Off the album titled ‘Utamu wa Yesu’, this song carries a powerful message that there is a lot of joy and ‘sweetness’ in Christ. This message is clear even through the dance moves which manifest that the people are really having fun in Christ. This song will have you dancing for Christ anytime.

3. KiatuKivue - Anastacia Mukabwa and Rose Muhando

Subscribe to watch new videos

This song is collaboration between Anastacia Mukabwa and Rose Muhando. You are likely to hear it in any Tanzania gospel music mix. Its message is based upon the story of Moses and the burning bush in the Bible; God commanded Moses to take off his shoes as he was approaching a holy ground. This song teaches us to shed off all earthly possessions and desires that may hinder us from hearing God’s voice in our lives. These two artists portrayed a lot of energy and passion in the song to bring out the vibrancy. The melodies are pleasant and carries a powerful message to all believers.

4. Ninang’ara - Christina Shusho

It is difficult to mention Swahili gospel music Tanzania without mentioning Christina Shusho. This female artist has over the years won the hearts of many fans especially across East Africa. ‘Ninang’ara’ is a song characteristic of top Tanzania gospel music. The artist sings about how she has shone and continues to shine in Christ. Basically, the Lord has made Christina shine and this is the message that she tries to put across throughout the song. Combined with strong vocals and wonderful video graphics, this is one of the best Tanzania gospel music songs. It will have you dancing, singing, and blessed.

5. Mavuno - Mireille Basirwa and Christina Shusho

‘Mavuno’ is a Swahili word that means harvest. It is collaboration between Mireille Basirwa and Christina Shusho. This song seeks to remind us that we will always have a harvest time for everything we plant. Thus, everyone is to reap what they sow, and it is better for one to sow good deeds to get a good harvest in the future. 6. Wakuabudiwa - Christina Shusho

‘Wakuabudiwa’ is a Tanzania gospel worship music song by Christina Shusho, which sets a prayerful mood. In this song, the artist proclaims that there is only God who is to be worshipped. This is an uplifting Tanzania Swahili gospel music melody that reminds us of the power of God and the need to worship Him always. By listening to this worship song, you will definitely feel the need to reconnect with God.

7. Mungu Gani - Rose Muhando

Subscribe to watch new videos

Rose Muhando took a hiatus for sometime from the gospel scene. However, she recently released an audio for her song ‘MunguGani’ which is one of the latest Tanzania gospel music songs. In this song, Rose reminds us that God still exists and to Him we should surrender our troubles. As Christians, we should resist thoughts about consulting other earthly gods or practices such as witchcraft as solutions to problems. This is such a good song especially because Rose Muhando’s vocals blend so well with the instruments and the beat. More so, it is great because it marks Rose Muhando’s comeback from her break. 8. Mungu ni Mungu Tu - Christopher Mwahangila

Subscribe to watch new videos

‘Mungu ni Mungu Tu’ is a song by Christopher a Tanzanian gospel artist. In this song, Christopher encourages people to always remember that there is God, no matter their circumstances. The message begins with Elijah’s story from the Bible, where the prophet sought to prove that God exists. Therefore, from this message, this artist reminds believers to always look up to God. He is always watching over them even when temptations and tribulations overcome them. Other than this inspirational message, the beat in the song is dance-able and the lyrics in the chorus are repetitive. These qualities make it an easy song to sing along to and learn.

9. Usifurahi Juu Yangu - Upendo Nkone

Subscribe to watch new videos

‘Usifurahi juu yangu’ is one of the gospel music songs from Tanzania done by UpendoNkone. The beat of this song is slow which almost makes it sound like a worship song. The slow beat, however, is what makes one curious to listen to the message in the song. Upendo Nkone tells her enemies not to be happy when she is weary and not to gossip when she is facing problems. This is because by so doing, these enemies would be inviting the devil to take control of their thoughts. Instead, they should pray for her well being, so that God can remember and bless them. The message in this song does not only apply to Upendo Nkone’s enemies, but to everyone in general. It is always better to wish your enemies well and pray for them in accordance to God's will. This message is definitely applicable to our daily lives and this song serves as a reminder of the same.

10. Nakaza Mwendo - Mt. Cecilia’s Catholic Choir Arusha

Subscribe to watch new videos

‘Nakaza Mwendo’ is one of the songs from the Tanzania choir gospel music scene. This song was done by Mt. Cecilia’s Catholic Choir of Arusha Tanzania. The message in this song is that they are keeping strong in the salvation journey. From the song, it is good for believers to always maintain their faith and be strong in salvation. The harmonic voices of the choir members in this song make it enjoyable to listen to it. This is definitely a great song for those who love catholic choir music. The message in the song is also uplifting and inspirational. This is why it made it to the list of top gospel music from Tanzania.

11. Waraka - Bahati Bukuku

Subscribe to watch new videos

This song by Bahati Bukuku has always been popular in the gospel scene. The song narrates the story of Esther in the Bible, who became a queen through God’s favour. The song is interesting especially because the video attempts to portray some of the scenes according to Esther from the Bible. Bahati’s vocals are really wonderful in this song. You need to listen to it so that you can remind yourself about Esther’s story in the Bible.
