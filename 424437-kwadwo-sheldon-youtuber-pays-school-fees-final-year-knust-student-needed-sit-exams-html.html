Kwadwo Sheldon: YouTuber pays School fees for Final Year KNUST Student to sit for Exams

 Posted on February 5, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  427 words  |  Lourie Helzer
  • Kwadwo Sheldon has been hailed by his fans and other Ghanaians on Twitter after he provided the deficit of a young man's school fees
  • The final year student at KNUST needed a top-up of GHc700 cedis (KSh 12,000) in order to sit for his examination
  • Sheldon saw the story which was shared by the KNUST Notice Board on Twitter and decided to pay the full amount

Popular Ghanaian YouTuber and host of Yawa of the Day, Kwadwo Sheldon, has paid GHc700 (KSh 12,726) to top up school fees of a final year KNUST student who was unable to sit for his final examination due to the deficit.

Sheldon made the kind gesture to Shadrack Ashong Martey after his story was shared on Twitter with many retweeting to get a helper for the young man to be able to write his papers and graduate.

In an almost similar case, students of the Kenya Medical Training College in Murang'a recently ran a funds drive around Murang'a town and collected over KSh 80,000 to help a colleague sit exams.

Salome Omutoko, a final year student risked missing exams due to school arrears amounting to KSh 116, 000, which had to be cleared before she was allowed into the hall.

Her friends explained that they decided to help Salome sit the exams because they had been colleagues for three and half years together and didn't want her left behind.
