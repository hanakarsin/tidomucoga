Thespian Tommy Dorfman has come out as a transgender woman after explaining that she was privately living as a woman for a year.

Tommy Dorfman comes out as a woman

The 13 Reasons Why star revealed her truth while doing an exclusive interview with TIME's magazine's novelist Torrey Peters.

“I view today as a reintroduction to me as a woman, having made a transition medically. Coming out is always viewed as this grand reveal, but I was never not out. Today is about clarity: I am a trans woman. My pronouns are she/her. My name is Tommy," he said.

The celebrity also explained that he has never felt safe talking about his coming out, but he keeps doing it regardless.

He added that he understood the transition as a beautiful thing and wanted the world to see what it looks like.

"So I kept, on Instagram, a diaristic time capsule instead—one that shows a body living in a more fluid space. I’m not changing my name. I’m named after my mom’s brother, who passed a month after I was born, and I feel very connected to that name, to an uncle who held me as he was dying. This is an evolution of Tommy. I’m becoming more Tommy," he added.
