Who is Sinach?

The Nigerian star Osinachi Egbu (famous known as Sinach) is an award-winning vocalist, songwriter, and one of the best transborder worship leaders of the moment. Being the songwriter, Sinach has written over 450 awe-inspiring songs from her heart. The most popular are “Jesus is Alive”, “Awesome God”, “For This”, “This Is my Season”, “I Stand Amazed”, “I Know Who I Am”, “The Name of Jesus”, “Simply Devoted” and a lot of others. These songs have turned into anthems in many homes and Churches all over the world.

Sinach’s music and writings have reached millions of people by fostering encouragement, hope, healing, and strengthening their belief.

Nigerian gospel music

African Gospel music is a kind of motivational Christian music that has become one of the main genre of Nigerian music. In the early 1960s the Evangelical Church of West Africa Choir was very popular, and in the 1970s Sola Rotimi, Bola Aare, Ebenezer Obey, Good women Choir, Panam Percy Paul, Lanre Teriba (ATORISE), Onyeka Onwenu, Asu Ekiye, Tope Alabi, Sammie Okposo, and Keffi were remarkable.

Furthermore, in the 1970s & 80s, the Christian Redeemed Voices and Archbishop Benson Idahosa's choir were famous for their gospel sound. The late Bishop acquired musical instruments for the choir, and all of them were gifted musicians. Also, he insisted that young adult church members and his student pastors take up musical instruments and play music. It was believed that they played instruments without any training and relied only on God’s gift.

There was a man known by the name of Rev. David Mporampora and a lady, Minister Dorothy James. Together they transmuted gospel music in Western Africa, and Nigeria particularly. Undoubtedly, they laid the foundation of the sound we hear these days.

A lot of West African students took up that sound and enthusiasm for the gospel. In the African Church of God Mission, the worship dance was founded. Thanks to the likes of Archbishop Idahosa, a man of God and of his people, the Nigerian gospel sound would never be the same.

Interesting facts about Sinach

Sinach, a key member of the Love World music team of Christ Embassy, began to sing when she was very young but couldn’t take it to the next level. In the beginning, she didn’t use her musical gift for gospel. However, when she met Pastor Chris Oyakhilome, a man of God, who taught her about the ways of the Lord, Sinach found her true calling.

Osinachi Kalu is from Ebonyi State, and she was born into a family of seven children where she is the second child. She graduated from the University of Port Harcourt, Rivers State, where she received a degree in Physics. She has divulged that her friend and mentor is Pastor of Christ Embassy, Chris Oyakhilome.

She loves to not only sing but to write as well. Usually, Sinach writes songs, poems, and books. By the way, her music has changed the lives of millions of people all around the world. This is because her songs preach repentance, healing, encouragement, and hope to people. Together with her Pastor, Rev Chris Oyakhilome, they have seen miracles live at various christian gathering including Healing school, crusades, and conventions all around the world.

Sinach has also led millions of people in worship and praise with awe-inspiring music concerts in the United Kingdom, Africa, North America, the United States of America, and many other countries. Also, she is the organizer of the worship concert “Sinach Live in Concert”, which took place in South Africa, Nigeria, Ghana, Zimbabwe, the USA, Cameroon, etc..

Is Sinach married? Who is her husband?

The gospel singer Osinachi Kalu (also known as Sinach) is married to her Christ Embassy’s Pastor Joe. As far as we know, her husband, Joseph Egbu, is a pastor in their church. They love each other very much and are a happy family blessed with one child. The couple love to be together all the time and always support each other.

Sinach has released several songs, and also won many awards. These days, she is one of the most gifted gospel singers in Nigeria, and she is very faithful to the ways of God. Actually, she is always present at Christ Embassy programs, and what is more, she has led praises in huge programmes such as Youth Conferences, Night of Bliss, the Christ Embassy Healing School, the Word at Work conventions, and many other programmes headed by Pastor Chris Oyakhilome.

Sinach music

Basically, Sinach has been given that heavenly opportunity to find herself, the outflowing of the Holy Spirit and witness astonishing miracles. She has released quite a number of great music and this number continues to increase. During her career, Sinach has gained several awards such as “Song of the Year” in 2008, the prestigious Love World Awards for “Songwriter and Vocalist of the year”, and also, she was awarded a trophy for “Nigeria Best Gospel Artiste” for 2012/2013.

Osinachi Kalu is a spiritually talented woman when it comes to Nigerian gospel music. Sinach has given hope to the hopeless all over the world through her awe-inspiring music.

So, in a nutshell, Sinach is a multiple award-winning songwriter, recording artiste, producer, and worship song leader. Also, this talented singer is a key member of Christ Embassy’s Love World music team. Despite the criticisms from her father, she started singing at a young age, and she has received pleasure by doing this.

From the very beginning, she knew that gospel music is not for commercial purposes. This kind of music is for helping people, to give the hope for hopeless, etc. Her gorgeous voice and awe-inspiring songs can help everyone who needs help. So, if you feel lost in your life just listen to Sinach music, maybe it will help you and bring some inspiration. Good luck!
