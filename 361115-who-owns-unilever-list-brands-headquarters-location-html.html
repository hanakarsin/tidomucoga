Unilever company profile

Unilever has a history that goes back to the 1880s where William Hesketh Lever founded the Lever Brothers Limited a family grocery business. Later on, he started a soap-making factory in Merseyside called Port Sunlight. The company grew and by 1890 Lever had soap factories around Europe, the USA, and Canada.

While Lever Brothers were creating their empire, two family-owned businesses Jurgens (margarine selling family business) and Van der Bergh (butter merchants) were also creating strategies to get ahead of their competitors. The two companies came together in 1908 to join forces and fight the weak margarine market. In 1927 Jurgens and Van der Bergh as well as Schicht, Centra and Jbusinesses formed Margarine Unie. This union was mainly composed of European businesses that were involved in the production of goods made mainly from oils and fats.

In 1929 Margarine Unie amalgamated with Lever Brothers Limited and on 1 January 1930 Univelever was officially formed. The company went on to release popular products such as Blue Band margarine, Dove, and Lifebuoy washing soap. While in the 1900s Unilever had over 50 product categories, it structured and focused on four main business areas: Personal care, home care, foods, and specialty chemicals.

Which country is Unilever from?

Want to know which country owns Unilever? Maybe you have been asking yourself is Unilever an Indian company? Or is Unilever an Israeli company? Well, it's neither of them. Unilever is based in two countries: England (Unilever PLC) and the Netherlands (Unilever NV). Hence Unilever headquarters are located in Rotterdam, Netherlands, and London, England.

Although Unilever is a dual-listed company consisting of Unilever NV in Rotterdam, Netherlands, and Unilever PLC in London, England, it operates under the same directors and as a single business. It's also a multinational company that operates in almost every continent.

This American based company solely deals with consumer foods and baking. Founded in 1913, it mainly operates in North America and Europe. The headquarters of Bestfoods is in Englewood Cliffs, New Jersey, USA.

This Unilever subsidiary has its headquarters in Mumbai, India. They deal with food, cleaning agents, personal care products, and even water purifiers. Its portfolio had 35 product brands as of 2019. Most of its items are mainly for the Asian market.
