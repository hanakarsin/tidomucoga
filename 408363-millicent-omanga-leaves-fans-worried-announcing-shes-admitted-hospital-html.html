Omanga's impressive story

The senator recently revealed that she made her first million at the age of 19 while still studying at the University of Nairobi.

The outspoken lawmaker said immediately she joined the institution after scoring B+ in high school, she ventured into the business of selling a range of merchandise, including bed sheets.

Omanga, also known as Mama Miradi, further divulged that no one gave her her business capital. She used the university tuition fee given to her by her parents to invest.

The Tanga Tanga allied lawmaker said she, at one point, got a tender from a hotel where she was required to supply bedsheets. She took a loan from a bank and delivered the supplies.

Mama Miradi said the hotel paid her one million in cash, which was how she made her first million.

Jubilee senator claims to be one of the biggest suppliers of bedsheets in Kenya.
