The musician’s fellow contestant and close friend Alvan love announced the bad news on his Facebook page.

According to Alvan, who was also admitted to a health facility, David’s condition went from bad to worse when he started convulsing numerous times in a day.

Alvan asked his fans to pray for David as he explained that the embattled artiste had been under the weather since Saturday, February 27.

“Guys help me pray for David Ogola D Major. Since Saturday morning he had to be taken to hospital after developing health complications. Four seizures in a span of six hours,” the radio presenter wrote.

According to Alvan, his pal requires constant monitoring by medics and can therefore no longer live in his house.

As previously highlighted on TUKO.co.ke, former Tusker Project Fame contestant David Major was finally discharged from rehabilitation after a few months under medical care.

Four months back, the singer was spotted living in the streets in very difficult situations, but he now looks like a new man who Kenyans once fell in love with.

Fellow TPF star Alvan Gatitu Love shared the good news on his Instagram noting David even gained weight during his time in rehab.

He said he is a totally different person from the man he saw months ago and God changed him for the better.

"Guess who is home from rehab? David Major, remember him? Just came out of rehab today and he's gained weight. There is a testimony because this man right here is not the man I last saw going away to rehab. God has surely changed him," he wrote.
