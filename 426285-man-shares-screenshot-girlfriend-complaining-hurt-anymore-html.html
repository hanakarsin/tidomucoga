Man Shares Screenshot of Girlfriend Complaining He Does Not Hurt Her Anymore

  • Abena Magis, a popular Ghanaian relationship advisor, recently shared the story of a young man who was confused about his girlfriend's requests
  • The lady had a conversation with her man, demanding that he should be a bit more rough and hurtful in order to keep the relationship thriving
  • Social media users have been sharing their opinions on the lady's attitude after reading her concerns

A young man in Ghana has amazed social media users after sharing a screenshot of a conversation he had with his girlfriend.

The picture that was sent to famous Ghanaian relationship advisor, Abena Magis, had the man anonymously inquiring whether the behaviour of his girlfriend was really normal.

"This is a screenshot of a convo between my girlfriend and me. Is it true that most ladies love it more when there is drama and petty fights in the relationship?" He asked.
