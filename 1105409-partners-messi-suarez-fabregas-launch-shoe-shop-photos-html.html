Partners of Messi, Suarez, Fabregas launch shoe shop (photos)

 Posted on February 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  244 words  |  Lourie Helzer

- The partners of Cesc Fabregas, Lionel Messi and Luis Suarez linked up to open a store in Barcelona

- The women were opening a shoe store in Barcelona in partnership with an Argentine fashion designer

- Messi's partner Antonella Roccuzzo owns the store with close friend, Sofia Balbi, Luis Suarez wife

Lionel Messi's partner Antonella Roccuzzo recently opened a shoe store with close friend, Sofia Balbi, Luis Suarez wife.

The two were joined by their footballer partners and Cesc Fabregas who came with his partner Daniella Semaan.

The show store which was opened in Barcelona was launched in partnership with an Argentine fashion designer.
