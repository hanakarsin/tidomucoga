The Bible has exciting stories that have lessons that assist believers to shape their decisions in their day to day life. Hollywood has, for the longest time, adapted Bible stories to make award-winning films that can be watched by families. Bible movies are among the most popular sub-genre of religious films for people who subscribe to the Christan faith.

Some of the oldest Hollywood movies based on the Bible include The Greatest Story Ever Told released in 1965, The Green Pastures (1936), and Noah's Ark (1928). Hollywood's film scene has evolved and diversified since the release of the first religious films.

Silent faith-based epic films are not as common today as they were back in the day. With the growth of platforms like Netflix and Hulu, one can be entertained by the many Bible films that have been produced over the years.
