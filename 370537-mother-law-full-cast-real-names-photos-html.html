Mother-in-Law is undoubtedly one of the most popular Television dramas in Kenya. The show airs on Citizen Television and has a massive fan base ranging from children to older men and women.

Since 2006, when it was first aired, the program has had a couple of talented actors and actresses gracing your screens. Some of these fantastic characters have left the show while others are still on. Interestingly, to many fans, their real names are a mystery.

Mother-in-Law Citizen TV series synopsis

Mother-in-Law is a weekly drama series that has been aired since 2006 on Kenya's Citizen TV. The family drama is divided into episodes, with each seemingly addressing a bundle of unique familial issues.

The television series is set in a typical family setting in one of the modern homes in Kenya. It demonstrates the existence of a middle-class Kenyan family. The storyline depicts the actions of a mother-in-law towards her husband, sons, daughter-in-law, and grandchildren. More importantly, the series has its eyes focused on the relationship between mother-in-law and her daughter-in-law.

Mother-in-Law cast real names

Who are these lovely Mother-in-Law actors and their real names? How much do you know about them? Most fans of this program do not know much about these characters, for instance, their real names. Here are the actual names of your favourite Mother-in-Law actors and actresses.

Charity - Elizabeth Wanjiru

Actress Elizabeth Wanjiru aka Charity Mwamba, has been on the sensational Television program for over ten years. The retired teacher is the main character and plays the conspicuous role of a mother-in-law.

The actress is a University of Nairobi graduate with a Bachelor of Arts degree and holds a postgraduate diploma.

Off the screens, Elizabeth Wanjiru is a proud mother of three children - two daughters and a son, and 11 grandchildren. The actress parted ways with her husband in 1981 after 13 years of marriage.

Mwamba - Peter Muriithi Maina

Peter Muriithi Maina is the charismatic, level headed father-in-law in this favourite television program. His acting journey commenced way back in primary school at Morison School, Bahati, in Nairobi.

He began acting seriously in the 1980s, and apart from Mother-in-Law, he has been actively involved in other famous plays such as Mama Benga, Pambazuko, and Naomi. In his outstanding role as Mwamba, Peter Muriithi Maina has been nominated twice, in 2009 and 2010 Kalasha Awards for Best Actor category.

Charlie - Peter Oketch Odhiambo

The flamboyant actor, who is popularly known for his love for liquor, is one of the main actors in the show. He features as Selina's husband and a father to Olive as well as a brother to Jack and Robert.

Selina - Catherine Kamau Karanja

Who is Selina of Mother-in-Law? She goes by her real name Catherine Kamau Karanja and plays as Charlie's wife and a step-mother to Olive. She has won the hearts of many fans acting in this capacity.

Olive - Idah Alisha Wanyoike

Idah landed the Olive role after doing a stage play dubbed There Goes The Bride, where she played as Polly Parkins alongside fellow Mother-in-Law counterpart Mustafa.

Mustafa - Andrew Muthure

Mustafa is the 'Leso' wearing character in Mother-in-Law. His actual name is Andrew Muthure. He has been acting the drama for over ten years now.

The talented actor was born in the low-income area of Kangemi but grew up in Kibera. He holds a Bachelor of Commerce degree in Marketing from Africa Nazarene University and was an assistant director of the popular Makutano Junction Television drama.

Rasta - John Githui

Rasta from Mother-in-Law is undoubtedly one of the most talented Mother-in-Law Citizen actors. He is in love with his boss's daughter Tina, and the pair have given the best to their fans as far as entertainment is concerned. They always come out powerful and victorious over their conflicts, making them the best couple on TV.

Tina - Jackie Mungai

Jackie plays the role of Tina - a no-nonsense sister to Mike and Angie. Who is involved in a secret and forbidden relationship with Rasta - a worker in their home.

Well, away from the screen, Tina is a teacher by profession. She schooled at Technical Primary School in Murang'a, then proceeded to Kahuhia High School, and joined Eregi Teachers Training College, from where she graduated with a teaching diploma.

Lisa - Faith Mary Nyaga

Lisa plays the role of a devoted wife of Robert Mwamba. She is one of the most recognizable faces on Television. She is one of the actresses in the show who have managed to keep their life private. However, other than being an actress, Faith Mary Nyaga is the mother of her lovely daughter called Turi.

Ninja - Jeff Okello

Who is Jeff Okello? Jeff is the actor who plays the role of Ninja (the watchman). He has, without a doubt, cut a niche for himself in the local entertainment scene. He was born in Huruma and raised in Kariobangi. Ninja is adored by many of his fans thanks to his charisma.

Who is the writer of Mother-in-Law Citizen TV?

Having looked at some of the talented Mother-in-Law actors and photos above, whom do you think is behind the entertaining show? Well, the Television show was scripted by one of its actresses.

Naomi Kamau aka Alisson, is the original scriptwriter. She is an "iron lady" of Kenya's entertainment industry and a force to reckon with, bearing in mind that she has several successful projects.
