The young man joined a day school to keep his musical journey going, something that he will have to multitask to ensure that no side is neglected.

Trio in school

In 2021, when the government reopened schools, the 17-year-old changed his look from an artiste to a student, something that many had not seen before.

The young man had shared a message on Instagram a day before going back to school, saying he would miss the outside world but promised more music in the future.

Now that he goes to a day school, he can maintain both sides of his life, and Kenyans should give him credit for what he has managed to achieve thus far.
