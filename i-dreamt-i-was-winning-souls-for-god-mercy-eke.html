I Dreamt I Was Winning Souls For God

 Posted on February 17, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  122 words  |  Enda Jantzen

Former Big Brother Naija housemate, Mercy Eke, has disclosed that she dreamt that she was winning souls for God and healing people.

The winner of the reality show disclosed this on her Snapchat story on Saturday.

Eke further stated that she neither lied nor drank alcohol on the eighth day of the year, as she asked God for more strength.

The 28-year-old added that she wants to be different this year.

She wrote, “Day 8: No alcohol, no lies, no sin, no party. God, I need more strength to keep going but outside is already calling my name.

“I want to be different this year. I had a dream where I was winning souls for God(I was preaching and healing people).”
