'RHOM' Star Lisa Hochstein Said She May Be Leaning on Her Boyfriend &quot;Too Much&quot;

Lisa Hochstein is hot and she knows it! The 'Real Housewives of Miami' star began dating someone new in late 2022. He appeared in Season 6's premiere.

The Gist:

  • Amid Lisa Hochstein's tumultuous divorce from Lenny Hochstein, she found love with someone new.
  • Lisa was first spotted with tech entrepreneur Jody Gidden late 2022.
  • Jody appeared for the first time on Real Housewives of Miami during the Season 6 premiere.

The divorce drama between Lisa Hochstein and her husband Lenny was messy, even by Real Housewives standards. Can you blame a girl for wanting to move on?

Well, the Real Housewives of Miami star did just that.

So who is Lisa Hochstein currently dating? Rumors first started swirling in late 2022 after the Bravo star was seen out with a handsome gentleman, while looking happier than she had been in a while.

Keep reading for the scoop about Lisa's dating life!

Who is 'The Real Housewives of Miami' star Lisa Hochstein dating? All about Joey Glidden.

Lisa Hochstein and Miami-based tech entrepreneur Jody Glidden first made waves when they were spotted together in mid-December 2022. They were then seen out celebrating NYE 2022 at a glamorous event at Miami Beach's Versace Mansion (because of course), but Lisa didn't confirm the relationship until February 2023.

Jody is the CEO of Postilize (a "software for cultivating customers via social AI," per Jody's Twitter) as well as Introhive (a company described as "the leader in relationship analytics").

In the past, Jody was the COO, CTO, and director of Chalk, a multimedia managing content company. And according to the Gossip Next Door, Jody's net worth is reportedly over $25 million dollars. (Yes, you read that right.)

Fans got to see Jody on their screens for the first time when he appeared in the Season 6 premiere of RHOM, which aired on Nov. 1, 2023. During the episode, Lisa mentioned that she'd been leaning heavily on her "very patient" man for support.

“Jody is such an incredible person,” Lisa said during the episode. “I’ve really been leaning on him — maybe too much. He’s been so supportive himself. He’s gone through a divorce. He’s also from Canada.”

“I don’t think I’ve ever had anyone look at me the way he does, ever, or love me this way,” Lisa also revealed during the premiere. “I tell him all the time he’s getting the worst of me right now. I’m like, ‘You just wait, 'cause if you like me now, you’re gonna love me when this is all said and done.’”

'RHOM' fans are happy for Lisa.

After Lenny filed for divorce from Lisa in May 2022, he hit her with a double-whammy by quickly moving in with his new girlfriend, before Lisa even really had time to process the breakdown of her marriage. Not only that, but Lenny basically said he wanted Lisa out of their home so his girlfriend could move in.

Can you blame Lisa for jumping back into dating after all of that drama and heartbreak?

Needless to say, the amount of supportive comments on social media for Jody and Lisa demonstrate we aren't the only ones that feel that way!

"Lisa and Jody do look really good together. Closer in age and both from the same place and they’ve gone through similar experiences. I’m happy she’s finally with someone that actually appreciates her! She deserves that. #RHOM," tweeted one fan.

"Seeing Lisa happy, I love it! It doesn’t seem forced or awkward like Lenny. And Jody can get it," tweeted another.
