Sean Bean - Age, Family, Bio

 Posted on June 10, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  147 words  |  Almeda Bohannan

Known for playing Ned Stark in the television series Game of Thrones and Boromir in the Lord of the Rings trilogy. He also played the villain Alec Trevelyan in the 1995 James Bond film GoldenEye. His other film credits include The Martian, Troy, National Treasure and Percy Jackson & the Olympians: The Lightning Thief.

Before Fame

He had a desire to become a soccer star but an accident with a glass door prevented him from pursuing it. 

Trivia

He is an acclaimed stage actor and appeared in many plays with the Royal Shakespeare Company. 

Family Life

He was married to Debra James from 1981 to 1988, Melanie Hill from 1990 to 1997, Abigail Cruttenden from 1997 to 2000 and Georgina Sutcliffe from 2008 to 2010. He has three children named Molly, Evie and Lorna. 

Associated With

He starred alongside Alexandra Daddario in the 2010 film Percy Jackson & the Olympians: The Lightning Thief.
